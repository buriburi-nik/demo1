Admission Process

Step-by-step guide to joining GPC Ziro

01

Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the minimum academic requirements for the diploma course you wish to apply for.

02

Fill Online Application

Visit the APDHTE admission portal and fill out the online application form with personal and academic details.

03

Entrance Exam (APJEE)

Appear for the Arunachal Pradesh Joint Entrance Examination (APJEE) conducted by the APDHTE.

04

Counselling & Seat Allotment

Qualified candidates must attend the counselling session for seat allotment based on their rank and preference.

05

Document Verification & Admission

Report to the college with original documents for verification and pay the admission fees to confirm your seat.
