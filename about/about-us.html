About Us

Institute Profile

Government Polytechnic College Ziro (GPC Ziro) is a Technical Education Institute under the Directorate of Higher and Technical Education, Department of Education, Govt. of Arunachal Pradesh. Started in the year 2025, it offers three years regular Diploma Engineering Programs in Civil Engineering (CE), Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE). It is approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education), New Delhi and Affiliated to the APSCTE (Arunachal Pradesh State Council for Technical Education), Itanagar.

The polytechnic college was established under the scheme “Sub-Mission on Polytechnics” of Govt. of India; aimed at setting up of polytechnics in the uncovered districts across the nation. The primary goal of the scheme was to expand the reach of Technical Education and skill development, especially in rural and disadvantaged areas, to enable local communities to obtain gainful employment. The scheme provided access to Technical Education, specially to aspirational districts for skill development.

The institute is located in the picturesque Ziro valley in the Lower Subansiri District of Indian state Arunachal Pradesh. It is located at Village Tajang, Jugo which is 6 kms from the Old Ziro market and 2 kms from the Siikhe Lake-a tourist attraction of the Ziro valley. Being away from disturbances of main city and located in a peaceful environment, GPC Ziro is an ideal place for learning with nature.
